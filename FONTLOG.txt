2.  Basic Font Information
Geo was drawn in July 1999. It was released without a license in 2001 (version 1.1).
From the current release, Geo is still available freely but its use, distribution and reproduction is now governed by the terms of the SIL Open Font License. A copy is included with this distribution: see the License file.

Family members:
Geo
Geo Oblique

You can read about Geo on my web site: http://www.readingtype.org.uk/
